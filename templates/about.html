About Us

Introduction

In the dynamic healthcare landscape, efficient information and resource management are vital for quality patient care. This project introduces a state healthcare-specific Health Management Information System (HMIS). This innovative HMIS centralizes patient data, employs AI/ML, and caters to various user roles, aiming to transform healthcare management.

Problem Statement

State healthcare facilities encounter issues with HMIS adoption, including low rates, fragmented data, inefficient record-keeping, limited predictive abilities, patient disengagement, and resource management. This project seeks to overcome these barriers and facilitate HMIS adoption by healthcare professionals.
